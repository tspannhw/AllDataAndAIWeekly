As we look forward in 2026, **SNACK-AI** is the architectural pattern for powering AI applications at any scale - from mobile phones and robots to massive enterprise clusters.

**SNACK-AI integrates:**
* **S**nowflake Platform
* **N**iFi (Apache)
* **A**pache Iceberg
* **C**ortex AI (Agents/Search/MCP)
* **K**afka (Apache Kafka)
